GeoClaw Developers' Workshop and Hackathon

May 16-18, 2025

www.clawpack.org/geoclawdev-2025


Description of the workshop

The workshop takes place May 16-18, 2025 at CU-Boulder facilities, and will focus on collaborations between core GeoClaw developers, their students, and users with new development needs.

It immediately follows the Community Surface Dynamics Modeling System CSDMS Annual Meeting (May 13-15) at CU. Several GeoClaw developers will be available during the meeting and on May 16 to assist current or potential users with any GeoClaw issues.

The Github repository clawpack/geoclawdev-2025 contains these webpages and can also be used for sharing resources.

The wiki in this repository can be used for suggesting projects (preferably with links to relevant Github Issues).

More details to appear.
